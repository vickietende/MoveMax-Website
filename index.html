About us

Welcome to Movemax

Movemax Car Rental is a local supplier of transport solutions centered on the car rental service. Our service offerings have been designed to ensure client edibility, comfort and convenience. We provide more than just traditional car rental services. We are a true local transportation solution for personal and corporate business needs. The company was established in January 2021 and offers the best competitive rates for self, chauffeur driven and airport shuttle. We also provide car services for special occasions such as conventions, meetings and weddings. If your business is not transport services, we are here to ensure that your transport needs are met so that you can focus on what you do best. We promise to do the same in meeting your needs.

Our Latest Services

Service at your whim

Our rental offer aims to save time and money. Our customized rental programs are designed specifically to meet your company’s needs. We will provide cars whenever, wherever and for whatever period you need the rentals.

Safe Travel

Our vehicles are comprehensively insured. Vehicle tools including wheel spanner, jerk and tyre rod are always inspected before vehicle checkout.

Customer Rate Plan

We offer straight forward, inclusive rate plans that make renting easy for our customers. For every rental whether it’s for a day, weekly, monthly or more. Our rates are customized to customer transportation needs.

A Sizeable Fleet

We have a wide selection of latest model vehicles that ensures you have access to the vehicles you need, including specialty vehicles such as SUVs, Saloon cars, single & double cab trucks and vans.

Chauffeur Driven

We offer Chauffer driven services if requested. Our drivers are trained in defensive driving.

24/7 Customer Service

We are available to provide customer service and support at all times even after business hours.
